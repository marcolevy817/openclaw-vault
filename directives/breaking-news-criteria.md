# Breaking News Criteria — High-Impact Filter

Only post to #breaking-news if a story scores **7 or higher** out of 10.

## Scoring Rubric

Score each factor and sum. Max = 10.

### Market Impact (0–4 pts)
- 4: Directly moves major markets (Fed rate decision, major CPI miss, bank failure, exchange halt, crypto exchange collapse)
- 3: Likely significant market reaction (earnings surprise >10%, major M&A, oil shock, major regulatory action)
- 2: Moderate market relevance (sector-specific news, commodity swing, index rebalance)
- 1: Tangential market connection
- 0: No market relevance

### Velocity / Breaking-ness (0–2 pts)
- 2: Breaking right now — unfolding in real time, not yet priced in
- 1: Just broke in last 1–2 hours
- 0: Already widely known / priced in

### Scope / Scale (0–2 pts)
- 2: Global or macro — affects entire economies, systems, or geopolitical order
- 1: National or major industry-wide
- 0: Regional, local, or niche

### Marco's Direct Relevance (0–2 pts)
- 2: Directly impacts one of Marco's active positions or strategies (ES/NQ futures, Polymarket, crypto, equities, options)
- 1: Relevant to Marco's watch areas or XComms business
- 0: Not relevant to Marco specifically

## Automatic 9–10 (Always Post Immediately)
- Federal Reserve emergency rate change
- Major exchange halt or circuit breaker triggered
- Bank run / bank failure (SVB-scale or larger)
- Sovereign debt default
- War declaration or major military escalation
- Major cyberattack on financial infrastructure
- Presidential/head-of-state death or incapacitation
- Crypto exchange collapse (FTX-scale)
- Major natural disaster affecting markets
- Pandemic-level health emergency declared

## Never Post (Score < 7)
- Regular earnings beats/misses within expectations
- Normal daily market moves
- Political commentary without direct market impact
- Sports, entertainment, lifestyle news
- News that's more than 3 hours old
- Anything already in the daily brief

## Output Format for #breaking-news

```
🚨 **BREAKING** — [Category]

[1-2 sentence summary of what happened]

**Impact:** [What markets/assets/sectors are affected]
**Why it matters to you:** [Direct relevance to Marco's positions or interests]
**Score:** X/10

[Source link]
```
